Privacy Policy for Beautyxia

Effective Date: 16/09/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Beautyxia (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Shopify app. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and transparently.

1Information We Collect

a) Face Photos

  • When you use the app to analyze your face, you may upload a photo.
  • Photos are processed temporarily to provide beauty tips and product recommendations.
  • We do not store or retain your photos after processing.

b) IP Address & Device Information

  • We may automatically collect your IP address and device type for security, fraud prevention, and service performance.
  • These logs are stored for a limited time (360 days) and then deleted.

c) Cookies

  • Our app uses essential cookies to function properly:
    • sessionid: Tracks your session securely.
    • csrftoken: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks.
    • language_preference: Remembers your chosen language (e.g., English or Arabic) so the app stays in your preferred language on reload (stored for up to 30 days).
  • These cookies are necessary for security, functionality, and language preference, and do not require consent.
  • We do not use non-essential cookies for analytics or tracking.

2How We Use Your Information

  • Face photos: To provide real-time beauty analysis and product recommendations.
  • IP/device information: For security, detecting abuse, and improving app performance.
  • Cookies: To ensure safe operation, maintain language preference, and functionality of the app.
  • Product tags: Tags added to products (like skin type or skin concerns) are used only to generate accurate recommendations for customers.

User Feedback (Likes/Dislikes)

  • After each analysis, users may optionally submit feedback by clicking a like or dislike button.
  • We only record the feedback type (like or dislike) and the date for internal statistics.
  • No personal data, IP address, or identifiers are stored with this feedback.

3Legal Basis for Processing

  • Face photos: Processed with your explicit consent when you upload them.
  • IP/device info: Processed under legitimate interest to maintain security and app functionality.
  • Cookies: Essential cookies are necessary for functionality and legal processing.
  • Product tags: Processed to provide recommendations under legitimate interest to enhance app functionality.
  • Feedback (likes/dislikes): Processed under legitimate interest to improve the app experience.

4Data Retention

  • Face photos are not stored.
  • IP/device logs are retained for 360 days and then automatically deleted.
  • Cookies remain for the duration of the session or up to 30 days for language preference.
  • Feedback (likes/dislikes) records are stored for internal analysis only and do not include personal data.

5User Rights

  • Access your data: You can request the IP/device info we have about you.
  • Request deletion: Ask us to delete your IP/device logs.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: houssaini.slimen@gmail.com

6Data Security

  • HTTPS for all data transfers.
  • Secure session handling via Django (sessionid and csrftoken).
  • Limited access to IP/device logs.

7Third-Party Services

  • Our app interacts with Shopify’s API to deliver its features.
  • We do not share any personal data with third parties beyond Shopify or as required by law.

8Shopify Data Deletion Requests

  • When a Shopify merchant uninstalls our app, Shopify may send us a data deletion request.
  • We will delete any related IP/device logs promptly.

9Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this policy occasionally.
  • Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
